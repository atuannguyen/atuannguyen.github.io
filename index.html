I am currently a DPhil student at the Department of Computer Science, the University of Oxford, working with Dr. Gunes Baydin and Prof. Yarin Gal. Before joining Oxford, I pursued Master Degree at Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology. During that time, I worked as a research assistant at KAIST's Machine Learning and Artificial Inteligence Group.

My research interests include Bayesian Deep Learning, Efficient Inference, Representation Learning, and Interpretable Machine Learning. I am also interested in facilitating Machine Learning for resource-constrained system (reducing network and data size).
